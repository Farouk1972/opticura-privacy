  <h1>Privacy Policy for OptiCura AI</h1>
  <div class="last-updated">Last updated: April 21, 2026</div>

  <p>OptiCura AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our eye health scanner web application.</p>

  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Email address</strong> – when you subscribe to our Pro plan, we collect your email to send invoices, renewal reminders, and important service notifications.</li>
    <li><strong>Eye scan images</strong> – the image you capture or upload is processed locally in your browser using Face‑API.js. We do NOT upload or store your images on any server.</li>
    <li><strong>Scan results</strong> – metrics like fatigue, redness, and risk scores are stored only on your device using localStorage. They are never transmitted to us.</li>
    <li><strong>Payment information</strong> – all payment processing is simulated for demo purposes. No real credit card data is collected. In a live version, payments would be handled by Stripe or PayPal, and we would not store your full payment details.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>2. How We Use Your Information</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>To provide the AI eye analysis service.</li>
    <li>To manage your subscription (send invoices, renewal reminders, and cancellation confirmations).</li>
    <li>To improve the app (anonymous usage statistics only – no personal data).</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>3. Data Storage & Security</h2>
  <p>All scan data remains on your device. We do not operate any backend servers that store personal information. Your email is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never sent to us unless you explicitly subscribe (demo simulation).</p>

  <h2>4. Third‑Party Services</h2>
  <p>Our app uses the following third‑party libraries, which may collect anonymous usage data according to their own policies:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Face‑API.js</strong> – runs entirely in your browser, no data leaves your device.</li>
    <li><strong>Chart.js & Tailwind CSS</strong> – no data collection.</li>
    <li><strong>Font Awesome</strong> – may collect anonymized font usage.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>5. Your Rights</h2>
  <p>You can delete all your stored scan data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (via DevTools → Application → Storage → Clear site data). You may also unsubscribe from the Pro plan by contacting us (simulated) or clearing your local subscription flag.</p>

  <h2>6. Children’s Privacy</h2>
  <p>OptiCura AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.</p>

  <h2>7. Changes to This Policy</h2>
